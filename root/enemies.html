Throughout history, countless members of the inferior gender have done terrible damage to our cause and hurt the stability of the patriarchy in America. Some of the most heinous perpetrators are below.

Susan B. Anthony: Susan B. Anthony was all bad things in life combined -- an abolitionist, suffragist, woman leader, author and speaker. By co-authoring the Declaration of Sentiments with the infamous Elizabeth Cady Stanton, together they claimed that “all men and women are created equal; that they are endowed by their Creator with certain inalienable rights,” undoubtedly triggering the worst movement in history thus far.

 

Margaret Sanger: Another of the Man-Up Society’s greatest enemies is Margaret Sanger a birth control activist, nurse, writer, and sex educator. She violated all moral decency when she set up one of the most horrendous places ever -- the first birth control clinic in the U.S. that evolved into today’s Planned Parenthood. We simply shudder at the thought.

Hillary Clinton : If she becomes our next president, all hell will break lose. Our beloved country will progress towards equal rights, which is an intolerable, heinous situation. In fact, she claims that she will

close the pay gap and fight for paid family leave. The horror.




We, the Man-Up Society, do not want to enforce political views, but please pick the candidate who will restore the patriarchy -- who will make America great again.

Alice Walker: Made “famous” by one of the most atrocious works in history The Color Purple, Alice Walker is a poet, novelist, women’s right activist, and civil rights activist who has hindered our vision of the future. Be wary of those like her -- knowledgable but wielding that knowledge for evil.

 

Sheryl Sandberg: Today’s biggest threat to all males everywhere is Sheryl Sandberg, COO of Facebook. She has used her power for evil as she continually brainwashes men to join the so-called feminist movement through books filled with propaganda like Lean In. She may have power and influence through one of the top Meninism companies in the world, but don’t let that fool you. Corruption is everywhere.
