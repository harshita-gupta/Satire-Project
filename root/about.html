Man Up is a non-profit organization that advocates for a societal and political return to a patriarchal world.

Through the recent development of “equal rights” movements, beginning with the passing of the 19th Amendment which allowed women to vote in America, the natural balance of order between men and women has been disturbed. The growth of the “feminazi” and “feminism” has risen exponentially, and feminazi groups have begun feeding propaganda to the world through social media networks and large news outlets.

Though they tout the idea of equal rights, their ideas are much closer to the definition of misandry than anything else.


The result is a growing conspiracy against men in the world. Man Up is a group which nurtures the return of the dominance of men. Our mission is highly supported by the facts of biology, psychology, and evolution. From birth, men and women are different are should be treated differently. The blatant differences in anatomy and hormonal levels between genders reinforce this idea. Men and women do and should have differing roles within work, relationships, sex, and society in general. Men should be treated as men; girls should be treated as girls.

Masculinity is distinct from femininity, and the characteristics should not overlap.




Man Up works to recognize the increasing problems for men in the world: declining rights to free speech, manipulation at the hands of women and the agenda pushed through their legislation, shaming of masculine culture, and more. Man Up speaks for all men when we say that we are done submitting underneath the current politically correct society that women abuse and use to control men. We are done keeping our mouths shut out of fear of being called “misogynists.” Such is not the way of the man. It’s time that men stood up and reasserted their natural and unalienable rights.

Thomas Jefferson once wrote, “We hold these truths to be self-evident that all men are created equal.” Let’s keep it that way.
