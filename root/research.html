Our Extensive Research on the Superiority of Men

James Maclern January 7, 2015 20 Tech 7.5

It is a well known fact that men are the superior sex.

Reliable research studies have shown that not only are male brains bigger,

our brains are also more developed in the areas requiring intelligence.


For example, when it comes to math, the brain of an 8-year-old boy SURPASSES the brain of a 12-year-old girl. This gap in intelligence simply widens as we get older: the older we get, the more developed and intelligent we are.

These proven scientific facts cannot be disputed--the sexes most certainly are NOT equal.



The only reason women are not scientists and engineers are because they

cannot keep up with their male coworkers’ smartness.


In fact, men are so smart that all of the research studies cited above were conducted by men.

Men are superior in other skills as well. Women are awful at reading maps, because they have less spatial perception. On the other hand, our superior spatial and depth skills are the reason why we are so much better at playing sports. The reason little girls don’t play sports is not because society tells them not to, as some misguided feminists have stated--they don’t play sports because they don’t have the skills to.

We are also stronger mentally--after all, women have higher rates of depression and anxiety.


Men, on the other hand, can tolerate pain that women couldn’t even imagine, according to Harvard professors.
